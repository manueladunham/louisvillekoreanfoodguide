Other

Other common Korean dishes

  • Kimchi

    pronounced kim-chee

    The most commonly known Korean dish consists of cabbage, radish, or other vegetables fermented with a variety of spices.

  • Bibimbap

    pronounced bee-bim-bop

    Rice served with a variety of vegetables and optional meat to be mixed with gochujang sauce.

  • Bulgogi

    pronounced bull-go-ghee

    Marinated beef served with rice can be cooked in the kitchen or on the grill at the table in some restaurants.

  • Japchae

    pronounced jop-chay

    Sweet-potato noodles mixed with beef and vegetables.

  • Galbi

    pronounced gall-bee

    Marinated beef short ribs.

  • Mandu

    pronounced mon-doo

    Steamed or fried dumplings containing almost anything from meat to vegetables.
